Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

Cashflow Forecast Budget (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Cashflow Forecast Budget is a personal finance planning tool that allows users to manually enter financial information such as transactions, bills, and budgeting data. The App does not connect to bank accounts and does not provide financial, investment, or tax advice.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

What This Policy Covers

  • What information is collected
  • How information is stored
  • Whether information is shared
  • Your rights and choices regarding your data

Important Notice

The App stores financial data locally on your device. The developer does not have access to your financial records.

Information We Collect

Cashflow Forecast Budget collects only the information that you voluntarily provide within the App.

Financial Data (Stored Locally)

  • Transaction amounts, dates, categories, and notes
  • Bill names, due dates, and recurring schedules
  • Budget goals and planning data
  • Currency preference and payday settings

This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to the developer.

Subscription Information

If you choose to purchase a premium subscription, payment processing is handled securely by Google Play.

The App does not collect or store your credit card details. Subscription status information may be received from Google Play to unlock premium features.

Device Information

  • App version
  • Device operating system version

Data Storage and Security

All financial data entered into Cashflow Forecast Budget is stored locally on your device.

The developer does not collect, store, transmit, or have access to your financial records.

You are responsible for securing your device with appropriate protections such as a device passcode or biometric authentication.

Data Sharing

Cashflow Forecast Budget does not sell, rent, or share your personal financial information with third parties.

The only third-party service involved is Google Play, which may process subscription payments if you choose to purchase premium features.

Data Retention

Because data is stored locally on your device, the App does not retain copies of your financial records on external servers.

Your Rights and Data Deletion

  • Edit or delete transactions and bills within the App
  • Remove all stored data by uninstalling the App
  • Stop using the App at any time

Children’s Privacy

Cashflow Forecast Budget is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Financial Disclaimer

Cashflow Forecast Budget is a personal finance planning tool and does not provide financial, investment, tax, legal, or accounting advice.

Any forecasts or projections are estimates based on user-entered data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

The developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
